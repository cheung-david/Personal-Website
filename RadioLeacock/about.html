Welcome to the website for Radio Leacock, the special radio station from the TDSB. This radio station was created at Stephen Leacock Collegiate Institute in 1970 and is currently run by Mr. Richard Houghton and students of the club. Radio Leacock broadcasts live from Monday to Friday in the morning and during lunch. Not only do we provide our student body with entertainment, the hosts also benefit from this! Each show is done downstairs in our private Radio Leacock studio. We are equipped with computers, an Alan and Heath console, speakers, Pro Tools and Adobe Audition software. As each member hosts his or her show, experience is gained from interacting with all the diverse equipment.
